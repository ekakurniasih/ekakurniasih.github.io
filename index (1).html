I am received B.S in Marine Science and Technology from Bogor Agricultural University and Master of Science in Marine from Diponegoro University. With a background marine biology, I am studying marine biodiversity through metabarcoding for reef fish, shark and decapoda across Indonesia. 


My research interests in connectivity, bioinformatics, and using molecular techniques to support direct management and conservation purposes. I also part of research team on shark identification to build a comprehensive database of shark species from areas arond Save seas, Indonesia using genetic monitoring approaches via eDNA metabarcoding funded by Biodiversity Information Fund for Asia (BIFA). 
 
While most of my work is based on marine biodiversity, I was involved in socio economic program and responsible in creating the public outreach and educational activities.
